🌌 欢迎来到我的博客

你好！很高兴你能来到这里～这是一个我用来记录学习与创作的空间，希望在这里能遇到同样对探索世界充满好奇的你！我是 「中关村文理学院」天体物理学专业的本科生，对物理学与天文学有一些浅浅的理解，同时也是一个游戏爱好者，既喜爱3A大作的震撼体验，也享受小众独立游戏的独特魅力——甚至已经萌生了自己做游戏的念头！

📘 这里有哪些内容？

  • 物理与天文 🌠
    分享我的学习笔记和思考，内容涵盖物理和天文相关课程的知识点。如果你对这些领域感兴趣，或是正在学习相关课程，欢迎来一起交流！

  • 游戏评测 🎮
    这里记录着我的游戏体验。与其说是评测，不如说是对游戏过程的点滴感悟，希望分享给同样热爱游戏的你。无论是沉浸式的3A大作还是创意十足的小品游戏，这里总有我的一份心得。

  • 实用小技巧 🔧
    这里偶尔会有一些小贴士，比如配置工具、解决报错问题之类的内容。或许不算深入，但希望能为遇到同样问题的你提供些帮助。

📚 如何查找内容？

每篇文章都归类整理，点击分类标签即可找到不同领域的内容。希望在这里，你能找到自己感兴趣的主题，也期待你留下宝贵的见解！

📬 联系我

如果有任何问题或建议，欢迎通过邮箱或GitHub与我联系，我会尽力解答。希望你在这里收获有趣的灵感，享受每一次阅读的过程！

感谢你的到访，祝你在此度过愉快的阅读时光！

🌌 Welcome to My Blog

Hello, and welcome! I'm glad you're here. This blog is a place where I share my learning notes, creative ideas, and inspirations.
I'm an undergraduate student studying astrophysics at Peking University. I have a budding passion for physics and astronomy,
and I'm also an avid gamer who loves everything from immersive AAA titles to unique indie games. Recently, I've even started considering making my own game!

📘 What You'll Find Here

  • Physics & Astronomy 🌠
    I share my study notes and thoughts on various topics related to physics and astronomy. Whether you're also a student in these fields or simply curious, I hope you'll find something interesting here!

  • Game Reviews 🎮
    Here, I document my experiences in the games I play. Rather than formal reviews, these are more personal reflections on the games I love. Whether it's an epic AAA title or a small indie gem, I hope to share the joy of gaming with you.

  • Useful Tips & Tricks 🔧
    Occasionally, I post some tips, like how to configure tools or troubleshoot errors I’ve encountered. These may not be deeply technical, but I hope they can help those facing similar issues.

📚 How to Find Content

Each post is organized by category, so you can click on tags to explore topics you’re interested in. I hope you’ll find something that speaks to you, and I'd love to hear your thoughts.

📬 Connect with Me

If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to reach out via email or GitHub. I’ll do my best to help.
Enjoy exploring my blog, and I hope it brings you some inspiration!

Thanks for visiting, and happy reading!
